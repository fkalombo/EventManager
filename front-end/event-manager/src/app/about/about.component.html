About Us

Welcome to the JSt EVENTS platform!

We are a group of 5 aspiring Software Developers who embarked on a 4 day
journey to create a (for the most part) functioning events management platform.

The aim was to create an interactive, easy to navigate platform.
Events hosts share event details with prospective attendees, attendees may
browse upcoming events and purchase tickets to secure their spots.

It has been a rewarding journey and we would like to thank BBD for the
opportunity to grow as developers and expand our skills.
We are are excited to see what the next few months hold!
